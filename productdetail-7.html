A28R PRO

The A28R PRO is a remarkable aluminium window system designed to deliver a striking visual presence along with a unique tactile experience. Combining elegance, innovation, and precision engineering, this system brings together the benefits of a window and a railing in one seamless solution.

The defining feature of the A28R PRO is its intelligently integrated railing within the window itself. This innovative design enhances safety while maintaining an open and unobstructed view, making it an ideal choice for balconies, French windows, and elevated openings.

Crafted by expert engineers using high-quality aluminium, the A28R PRO delivers excellent structural stability while retaining an exceptionally slim profile. Its refined construction ensures durability, safety, and smooth functionality over extended periods of use.

Aesthetically, the A28R PRO stands out with its ultra-slim design and clean lines. The system is designed to maximize transparency, allowing abundant natural light to enter the space while preserving a sleek and contemporary architectural look.

Engineered for modern living, the A28R PRO combines safety and comfort without compromising design. Its integrated railing solution provides confidence and security while maintaining the elegance of floor-to-ceiling window installations.

Designed with performance and ease of use in mind, the A28R PRO offers smooth operation, precise engineering, and long-lasting reliability. The system is suitable for residential and premium architectural applications.

Exceptionally slim and thoughtfully engineered, the A28R PRO is considered one of the thinnest Juliet window systems available. It redefines modern window design by blending safety, elegance, and innovation into a single refined solution that elevates contemporary living spaces.

Designed to push the boundaries of modern architectural design, the A28R PRO delivers a perfect harmony of transparency, safety, and refined aesthetics. Its ultra-slim aluminium profile, combined with the integrated railing concept, creates a bold yet minimal statement while ensuring confidence at elevated openings. The A28R PRO is not just a window system, but a distinctive architectural feature crafted to enhance contemporary homes with elegance, innovation, and lasting performance.
